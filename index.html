From Newsroom → Taproom

At the turn of the 20th century, Franklin Gothic shouted the stories of modern life from newsstands and billboards. PRESS BREW captures that same industrial confidence in a 750 ml bottle — steeped, pressed, and aged to let its character resonate.

Bold Flavor

Dark malt & subtle citrus mirror the heavy strokes and open counters of Franklin Gothic — a complex balance that finishes clean.

Silent Resonance

Like the typeface’s enduring legibility, the flavor reveals itself quietly, lingering as an elegant after‑note.

Letterpress Legacy

Each batch is numbered with vintage metal type, hand‑pressed in a 1900s print‑shop to keep the craft alive.
